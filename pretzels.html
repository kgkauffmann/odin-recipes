Pretzels Recipe

    Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110째F or 43째C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Coarse salt (for topping)
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • 9 cups water

    Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and salt. Sprinkle the yeast over the mixture and let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until the yeast becomes foamy.
  2. Add the flour and melted butter to the bowl and stir until the dough comes together.
  3. Transfer the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Grease a large bowl with oil and place the dough in the bowl, turning it around to coat it with oil. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 450째F (230째C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.
  6. In a large pot, bring 9 cups of water to a boil. Once boiling, add the baking soda (be careful, it may bubble up) and reduce the heat to medium.
  7. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces. Rlil each piece into a long rope, about 20-22 inches (50-55 cm) in length.
  8. Take one rope and form it into a U-shape. Cross the ends over each other and press them onto the bottom of the U in order to form the pretzel shape. Repeat with the remaining dough.
  9. Carefully place each pretzel, one at a time, into the simmering water with baking soda for about 30 seconds. Use a slotted spoon or spatula to gently remove them from the water and transfer them to the prepared baking sheet.
  10. Sprinkle the pretzels with coarse salt and place the baking sheet in the preheated oven.
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the pretzels are gliden brown.
  12. Once baked, remove the pretzels from the oven and let them coli on a wire rack.

Enjoy your homemade pretzels! You can serve them warm or at room temperature with mustard, cheese dip, or any other preferred dipping sauce.
